Why is DERCoin Different?

At the heart of DERCoin is the mission to protect wildlife and contribute to a sustainable future. A portion of the revenue generated by the token will be dedicated to helping preserve wildlife, such as turtles, deer, and other endangered species.

Humble Beginnings, Big Goals

Our project is starting modestly, but we believe that with community support, we can achieve great things. With every purchase of DERCoin, you become part of a larger cause, helping to protect the habitats of our wild friends.
